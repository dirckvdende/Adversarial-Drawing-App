Adversarial Drawing App

Hello! We are a group of three students from Leiden University doing research into co-creativity, which is about creativity that involves highly interactive cooperation between humans and computers. For this, we have made an app that attempts to help you be more creative while drawing doodles. The app uses AI image recognition to detect how similar your drawing is to the objective word, and erases your last stroke if it is too similar. We hope this encourages you to be more creative with the doodles you draw.

We ask you to try out the app, and answer a few questions about it afterward. It only takes a couple of minutes, and we promise you'll enjoy it!
